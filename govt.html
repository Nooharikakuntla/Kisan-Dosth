Kisan Dosth- AgriGov: Your Path to Prosperity

AgriGov is your gateway to a wealth of government schemes dedicated to Indian agriculture and farming.
Explore an array of innovative initiatives, from crop insurance to sustainable practices, designed to boost your agricultural journey.
Discover, apply, and prosper in the world of farming. Your path to prosperity begins here.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

This scheme provides direct income support to small and marginal farmers by giving them Rs. 6,000 per year in three equal installments.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

PMFBY is a crop insurance scheme aimed at providing financial support to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

National Agriculture Market (eNAM):

The eNAM is an online platform that connects agricultural produce markets across India, enabling farmers to sell their produce electronically and get the best prices.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC):

The Kisan Credit Card scheme provides farmers with a credit card to access short-term credit for their agricultural and related needs at subsidized interest rates..

Soil Health Card Scheme:

This program provides farmers with soil health cards containing information about soil quality and recommendations for appropriate crop selection and fertilizer use.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY):

PMKSY focuses on improving the efficiency of water use in agriculture through the development of water resources and efficient water management practices.

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY):

This scheme supports the implementation of agricultural development projects at the state and district levels to enhance agricultural productivity.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY):

PKVY promotes organic farming by providing financial assistance to farmers for the adoption of organic farming practices.

Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization:

This program encourages the adoption of modern farm machinery and equipment to enhance productivity and reduce the drudgery of manual labor.

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA):

NMSA aims to promote sustainable agriculture practices by supporting soil health, water use efficiency, and climate-resilient farming.

Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA):

This scheme aims to ensure remunerative prices to farmers for their produce and minimize price fluctuations.

Market Intervention Scheme (MIS):

MIS is implemented to provide price support to farmers in case of a fall in market prices for their agricultural produce.

National Mission on Oilseeds and Oil Palm (NMOOP):

NMOOP promotes the cultivation of oilseeds and oil palm to reduce India's dependence on oil imports.

National Food Security Mission (NFSM):

NFSM aims to increase the production of rice, wheat, pulses, and coarse cereals to ensure food securit

National Horticulture Mission (NHM):

Supports horticultural development, including the cultivation of fruits, vegetables, and flowers.

National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA):

Promotes climate-resilient farming practices and sustainable agriculture.

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sinchai Yojana (PMGSY):

Focuses on improving irrigation facilities in rural areas to enhance agricultural productivity

Livestock Insurance Scheme:

Provides insurance coverage to livestock to protect farmers from economic losses due to livestock mortality.

Blue Revolution:

Promotes fisheries and aquaculture development, including the establishment of fish farms.

Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS):

Supports the establishment of small dairy units to enhance milk production and income for farmers.

National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET):

Promotes agricultural extension services and technology transfer to farmers.

Agriculture Insurance Schemes:

Various state-level and national crop insurance schemes to protect farmers from crop losses due to natural calamities.

Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centers (ACABC):

Supports the establishment of agribusiness centers and agricultural clinics for training and services.

Schemas Provided By
Govt of Telangana

Rythu Bandhu:

The Rythu Bandhu scheme provides financial assistance to farmers in the form of investment support for the purchase of seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs. It is designed to support farmers before the Kharif and Rabi seasons.


Rythu Bima:

This scheme offers life insurance coverage for farmers, and in the event of a farmer's death, their nominees receive a financial benefit. The premium for this insurance is paid by the state government.


Rythu Vedika:

Rythu Vedika is a farmer coordination and service delivery platform aimed at providing a range of services and information to farmers. It acts as a community center for farmers.


Mission Kakatiya:

This project focuses on the restoration of minor irrigation tanks, lakes, and reservoirs to enhance water availability for agricultural purposes.


Mission Bhagiratha:

Although primarily aimed at providing safe drinking water, this scheme indirectly benefits agriculture by ensuring better access to water for irrigation.


Telangana State Seed Development Corporation (TSSDC):

TSSDC provides high-quality seeds to farmers at reasonable prices.


Telangana State Agriculture Development Corporation (TS Agros):

TS Agros supports farmers by procuring and marketing their agricultural produce.


Double Bedroom Housing Scheme for Farmers:

this scheme aims to provide quality housing for farmers by constructing double-bedroom houses


Sheep Distribution Scheme:

This scheme aims to improve the livelihood of rural communities by distributing sheep to beneficiaries, particularly women, to support income generation.
